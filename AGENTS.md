# Errday product direction

For every change that affects the web or iOS interface, use the `cleanui`
skill before editing UI code.

- Build a calm, native-feeling health tracker, not a marketing page or an AI demo.
- Prefer a single clear primary action per screen and short, factual labels.
- Keep the dark grey and violet palette restrained. Do not add glow effects,
  oversized shadows, decorative badges, or generic motivational copy.
- Use consistent compact radii, subtle borders, accessible touch targets, and
  real icons from the platform's icon library.
- Design mobile first. Avoid horizontal overflow and verify key screens at
  phone and desktop widths.
- Keep AI optional and secondary. Never make it the visual centre of a normal
  tracking flow.
